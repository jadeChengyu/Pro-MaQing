Academic Profile

Professor Angel Qing Ma is a Professor in the Department of Education Policy and Leadership at The Education University of Hong Kong. Her research expertise spans educational policy, comparative education, higher education management, and social inequality in education.

With extensive academic experience and international influence in comparative education policy analysis, Professor Ma's work focuses on critical issues including globalized educational governance, internationalization of higher education, educational equity, and social mobility.

Her research outputs demonstrate both theoretical depth and practical relevance, making significant contributions to policy development and academic discourse in the field.
